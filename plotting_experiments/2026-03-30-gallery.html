We made a frankly unreasonable number of plots to figure out which visualizations of the CLAM matrix and ERICA's replicability metrics are most useful for understanding internal cluster structure. All analyses use ERICA v0.1.4 with B=200 MC iterations, 80/20 train-test split. Primary methods: K-Means and Agglomerative Clustering (Ward and Single linkage). Figures generated with matplotlib; viridis and Okabe-Ito colorblind-accessible palettes. Total figure inventory: 612 figures across all pages (80 in this gallery, 363 by-dataset breakdowns, 101 entropy/CRI playground figures, 68 ERICA statistic deep-dive figures).

*HDBSCAN integration is under active development. We included the figures because they exist, but we are not yet confident enough to make eye contact with a reviewer about them.
