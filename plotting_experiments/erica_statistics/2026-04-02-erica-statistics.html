ERICA Statistics — Exploratory

Extending Parmigiani's ARI/AMI to Monte Carlo subsampling, then throwing plots at the wall
2026-04-02
ERICA statistic = max(CLAM[i,:]) / sum(CLAM[i,:]). Per-sample assignment consistency. Range: 1/K to 1.0.
ERICA-ARI = per-iteration ARI(predicted_test, refit_test). Partition-level agreement. Range: -1 to 1.
ERICA-AMI = per-iteration AMI(predicted_test, refit_test). Information-theoretic variant. Range: 0 to 1.
Adapted from Parmigiani et al. (2023) Algorithm 1 to Monte Carlo subsampling (B=200).
