This gallery compares ERICA visualizations against figures from the clustering stability literature. All analyses use ERICA v0.1.4 with B=200 MC iterations, 80/20 train-test split. Datasets include VDX breast cancer (3-gene subset), Gaussian sigma sweeps, and 2D shape datasets. Click any figure to enlarge.

Assignment Similarity Matrices

Sample × sample matrices showing assignment profile similarity. Computed as the inner product of L1-normalized CLAM rows. Reordered by hierarchical clustering to reveal block-diagonal structure. Inspired by Monti et al. (2003) consensus matrices.

Note: This is the CLAM-derived co-assignment similarity (inner product of normalized assignment vectors), not the Monti consensus matrix. The Monti matrix conditions on pairwise co-occurrence in the same test subsample (diagonal = 1.0); our computation measures similarity of assignment profiles (diagonal < 1.0 for uncertain samples). Both reveal block-diagonal structure for well-separated clusters.
